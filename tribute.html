Nicolino Locche

Nicolino after one of his many wins.

A couple of things about Nico:

  • Argentinian professional boxer.
  • He held the World Light welterweight title from 1968 to 1972.
  • Locche was a defensive genius.
  • In 2003 inducted into the International Boxing Hall of Fame.
  • His nickname was "El Intocable" ("The Untouchable").
  • Debuted at the age of 9 and his amateur record was 117-5.
  • Turned professional at the age of 19.
  • He used to smoke cigarettes and his diet was very flexible.

His record:

  • Total fights: 136
  • Wins: 117
  • Wins by KO: 14
  • Losses: 4
  • Draws: 14
  • No contests: 1
